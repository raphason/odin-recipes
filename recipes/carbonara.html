Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

A traditional Spaghetti Carbonara, a great source of carbs, protein, and deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta
  • Parmesan
  • Garlic
  • Salt + Black Pepper
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. In a boiling pot of lightly salted water, cook the spaghetti until al dente. Drain and reserve 1/2 cups of water.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together eggs and Parmesan; set aside.
  3. Heat a large skillet or pan over medium-high heat and cook the pancetta until lightly browned. Save excess fat.
  4. Add in garlic over low heat.
  5. Lightly stir in egg and parmesan mixture into pasta, generously seasoning with salt and pepper, and adding pancetta and garlic once complete.
  6. Garnish with parsley and serve.
