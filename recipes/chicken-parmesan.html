Chicken Parmesan

Description

Evenly breaded, tomato sauce and mozzarella coated chicken parmesan is a dish everyone can enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Panko Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Basil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 450ËšF.
  2. Prepare Chicken Breasts by firmly pounding with a meat mallet on a flat surface.
  3. Beat eggs in one bowl and mix breadcrumbs and parmesan in another.
  4. Using flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, bread the chicken.
  5. Cook chicken over olive oil in skillet until golden, about two minutes each side.
  6. Transfer chicken to baking dish and cover with tomato sauce, mozz, and basil.
  7. Bake in oven for 15 to 20 minutes.
