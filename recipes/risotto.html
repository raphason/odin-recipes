Risotto

Description

Requiring love and care, this risotto will taste as good as the attention that you give to its preparation.

Ingredients

  • Arborio Rice
  • White Wine
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Butter/Olive Oil
  • Onion
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper

Directions

  1. Bring pot of water to a medium-high heat simmer.
  2. Cook onion over medium heat in olive oil, adding water until it evaporates and onion is nice and tender.
  3. Cook rice in oil in pot until lightly translucent. Then, add wine and simmer until evaporation.
  4. Over medium heat, add salted water to rice in 3/4 cup increments, stirring constantly and adding water as needed until rice is al dente.
  5. Take pot off heat, add butter, and mix until melted.
  6. Serve risotto in bowls with added parmesan and black pepper.
